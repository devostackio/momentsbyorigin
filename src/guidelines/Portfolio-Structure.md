# Origin Portfolio Structure

This document outlines the four distinct companies under the Origin umbrella and their corresponding website designs.

---

## Company Overview

### 1. Origin Creative Group (Layout 1)
**Role:** Umbrella Company / Investment & Community Building

**Branding:** 
- Main name: "ORIGIN"
- Subtitle: "Creative Group"

**Focus:**
- Vision and mission statement
- Investment opportunities and community outcomes
- Local city presence and neighborhood impact
- Portfolio of child companies and ventures
- Youth development programs
- Relationship-building and partnerships

**Design Style:** 
- Classic editorial hero with gradient
- Muted, nostalgic aesthetic
- Focus on community impact and stats
- Features cards for each child company

**Target Audience:** 
- Investors
- Community partners
- Local businesses
- Civic organizations

## Shared Brand Elements

### Common Across All Properties:
- **Parent Brand:** ORIGIN (always in caps)
- **Typography:** Playfair Display (serif) + Inter (sans-serif)
- **Core Colors:** 
  - Charcoal (#2B2B2B, #3A3A3A, #4A4A4A)
  - Canvas (#F5F5F3)
  - Accent Rose (#C4968A)
  - Accent Terracotta (#B88A7D)
  - Accent Sage (#9CAA98)
  - Accent Denim (#7B8B9E)
  - Accent Sand (#D4C5B0)

### Navigation Between Properties:
Each site should link to the other properties in the Origin ecosystem, allowing users to discover the full portfolio.
